About Launch Lab

Exploring aerospace through missions, simulations, and curiosity.

🚀 What is Launch Lab?

Launch Lab is an interactive aerospace website where students can explore how rockets and spacecraft work through missions, simulations, and challenges.

Instead of simply reading facts, users can experiment with decisions, test ideas, make mistakes, and discover why a mission succeeds or fails.

✨ Why I Built It

I've been fascinated by space and rockets for years. As I learned more about aerospace, I noticed that many resources were either very technical or focused mostly on memorization.

I wanted to create something more interactive, a place where students could actually experience aerospace concepts instead of only reading about them.

My goal is to make space exploration feel exciting, approachable, and fun for curious learners.

👩‍🚀 Meet the Creator

Hi! I'm Tanvi, a high student who loves aerospace engineering, space exploration, and building creative projects.

Launch Lab began as a personal project when I decided to learn web development and create an aerospace platform from scratch. What started as a simple rocket simulator is gradually growing into a collection of missions, simulations, and interactive experiences.

🌕 What's Next?

Future plans for Launch Lab include adding a learning section, Moon Landing missions, Mars exploration challenges, rocket staging simulations, orbital mechanics activities, and many more interactive aerospace experiences.
